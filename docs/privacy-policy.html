Privacy Policy

2048 Puzzle Game — com.game.puzzle2048

Overview

2048 Puzzle Game ("the App") is a free, offline puzzle game developed by MKG Games. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects (or does not collect) and how it is handled.

Data Collection

This App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Specifically, the App does NOT:

  • Collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers
  • Track your location or device information
  • Use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services
  • Contain any third-party SDKs that collect data
  • Display advertisements
  • Require an internet connection
  • Require user accounts or login

Local Storage

The App stores your best score locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is never shared with any third party
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App

Internet Access

The App does not require or use internet access. It is a fully offline game. No network permissions are requested.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children. It is suitable for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, APIs, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Significant changes will be reflected in the App's store listing.
